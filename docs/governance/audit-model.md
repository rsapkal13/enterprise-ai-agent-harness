# Audit Model

Audit records should connect requests, identities, skills, policies, tools, workflows, systems, decisions, and outcomes.

The audit model should answer:

- Who or what requested the action?
- Which skill was invoked?
- Which policies were evaluated?
- Which tools and systems were used?
- What changed?
- Was approval required?
- What was the final outcome?

Audit records are governance evidence. They should be structured enough to reconstruct the journey without exposing unnecessary sensitive data.
